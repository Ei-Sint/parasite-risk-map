⚠ Assumptions & Limitations

What this tool does

This prototype visualises indicative parasite risk levels for UK farm and pasture locations based on current environmental conditions. It is a proof-of-technology — not a diagnostic or veterinary tool.

Risk Scoring Rules

Each farm receives a risk score (0–10) calculated from four factors:

FactorRuleMax Points
Temperature≥10°C = +1, ≥15°C = +2, ≥20°C = +33
Rainfall (7-day)≥5mm = +1, ≥15mm = +2, ≥30mm = +33
Humidity≥60% = +1, ≥75% = +22
SeasonSpring/Autumn = +2, Summer = +1, Winter = 02
Total → LOW (0–3), MEDIUM (4–6), HIGH (7–10)10

Data Source

Weather data is fetched in real time from Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — a free, open-source weather API. Data is refreshed each time the page loads.

Limitations

• Risk scores are based on simplified rules and should not replace veterinary advice.
• Farm locations are representative examples, not a complete database.
• Rainfall shown is the total for the past 7 days from the nearest weather station.
• Parasite risk is also influenced by soil type, stocking density, and treatment history — none of which are currently modelled.
• This tool has not been validated against real parasite burden data.
